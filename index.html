About Me

My name is Guy Bracha, a 27-year-old software engineer from Herzliya, Israel.
I recently graduated in Software Engineering from a technical college, where I gained hands-on experience in designing and developing modern applications.
I have a strong passion for computers, gaming, and web development, and I enjoy combining creativity with technology to build engaging digital experiences. Over the past few years, I have worked on diverse projects ranging from full-stack web applications to interactive front-end interfaces, often using React, Node.js, Firebase, and cloud technologies.
In addition to my technical skills, I’m curious and constantly exploring new tools and approaches — including AI technologies such as GPT and Midjourney — to enhance creativity, speed up workflows, and deliver unique results.
What excites me most is the opportunity to learn, adapt, and grow while contributing to meaningful projects. I’m highly motivated, detail-oriented, and driven to bring value both independently and as part of a team.
Beyond my professional work, I also enjoy creating comics and drawing in my free time, which allows me to express creativity visually and bring imaginative ideas to life.
